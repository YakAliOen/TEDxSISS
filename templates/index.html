What is TEDx ?

x = independently organized event

In the spirit of ideas worth sharing, TEDx is an international network of over 2,000 local, self-organized TED-style events. Their programs include a mix of live speakers, TED videos, and demonstrations. The TED conference provides general guidelines for their program, but individual TEDx events are the result of the work of a local team of volunteers.

What is TED?

TED is a nonprofit organization dedicated to ideas that change everything. Started as a four-day conference in California 41 years ago, TED has grown globally and supports its mission with a variety of initiatives. At its annual conferences, TED invites the world’s leading thinkers to speak about their ideas. Their talks are recorded and posted for free viewing on TED.com.


The annual TED conference is held each spring in Vancouver, British Columbia. TED's media initiatives include TED.com, where new TED Talks are published daily; the Open Translation Project, which provides subtitles and interactive transcripts, as well as translations by volunteers around the world; the educational initiative TED-Ed, and others.
